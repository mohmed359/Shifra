Start your journey in UI and UX design - from research to prototype. Hands-on projects, cutting-edge tools, and expert advice from expert instructors.

Ahmed Khalil

Senior Product Designer • 8 years experience

Ahmed worked with startups and large enterprises to build delightful user experiences. He focuses on research-driven design and pixel-perfect interfaces.

About this course

In this course, you will learn to conduct user research, design simple interfaces, create interactive prototypes, and conduct practical usability tests with applied exercises.

Curriculum

1. Introduction & Course Overview 10 min • 1 Lecture
2. User Research Methods 45 min • 3 Lectures
3. Information Architecture & Wireframing 45 min • 3 Lectures
4. Visual Design & Components 52 min • 5 Lectures
5. Prototyping & Usability Testing 1 hr • 3 Lectures

What you'll learn

  • User Experience (UX) Design Basics
  • Designing User Interfaces (UIs) with Figma
  • Transforming Research into Testable Interfaces
  • Building and Evaluating a Prototype with Users
